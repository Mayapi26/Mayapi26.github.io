About Out of the Box

We are a tech innovation company passionate about creating solutions that make Mexico better. Our mission is to bridge generational gaps in technology teams and accelerate innovation through structured collaboration.

Founded on the principle that diverse perspectives drive breakthrough innovations, we've developed the Innovation Loop Weeks platform to help companies harness the unique strengths of every generation in their tech teams.

Our Vision: A Mexico where every tech team operates as a unified innovation engine, regardless of generational differences.
